Before You Go

Walk Structure

Connection

Lead walking + calm sniffing. You reward when she checks back in or orients to you.

Controlled Freedom

Off lead. Let her explore but regularly call her back for a treat or praise before releasing again.

Hunting Work

Hide treats or toys. Encourage searching using nose work (scatter, hidden ball, grass searches).

Managed Freedom

Free movement but you interrupt every so often to get eye contact or recall and reward.

Reset

Slow sniffing, close proximity walking, calm behaviour only — reduce arousal.

Play

High energy reward games like ball, chase, retrieve. Use structured start/stop control.

Cooldown

Very slow walking, sniffing, and settling before finishing the walk.
